About Me:

I am interested in the areas of applied mathematics, mathematical modeling, optimization and machine learning. Over the past 6 years, I have applied these techniques to functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) and developed algorithms/software for the analysis of fMRI data.

I have created this page to:

  • make available links and pre-prints of some of the papers that I have published.
